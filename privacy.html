1 Who We Are

AssetTag is a mobile application for physical asset management developed and operated as an independent software product. When this policy refers to "AssetTag", "we", "us", or "our", it means the developer of the AssetTag application.

This privacy policy explains what personal information we collect when you use the AssetTag app, why we collect it, how we use it, and what rights you have in relation to it.

If you have any questions about this policy or your personal data, please contact us at: privacy@assettag.app

5 Where Your Data Is Stored

Your asset data

Your entire asset register ‚Äî all asset records, photos, audit logs, user accounts, and settings ‚Äî is stored in a single Microsoft Excel (.xlsx) file on your own cloud storage account:

  • OneDrive: stored in your personal or business Microsoft OneDrive
  • Google Drive: stored in your personal or business Google Drive
  • SharePoint: stored in your organisation's Microsoft SharePoint site

AssetTag reads this file when you open the app and writes to it when you make changes. The file never passes through or is stored on AssetTag's servers. Data residency is determined by where you have configured your Microsoft or Google account to store data.

Authentication tokens

OAuth tokens used to access your cloud storage are stored securely on your device using Android's encrypted keystore (via Flutter Secure Storage). They are never transmitted to AssetTag's servers.

Subscription data

Your subscription status is managed by RevenueCat and Google Play. RevenueCat stores your purchase history on servers in the United States. Their privacy policy is available at revenuecat.com/privacy.

Scan history

Your recent scan history is stored locally on your device only (in SharedPreferences) and is never transmitted externally.

8 Your Rights Under UK GDPR

As a UK resident, you have the following rights in relation to your personal data:

  • Right of access ‚Äî You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification ‚Äî You can ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ‚Äî You can ask us to delete your personal data. For your asset data, simply delete the Excel file from your cloud drive and uninstall the app
  • Right to restrict processing ‚Äî You can ask us to pause processing your data in certain circumstances
  • Right to data portability ‚Äî Your asset data is already in a portable Excel format in your own cloud drive
  • Right to object ‚Äî You can object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to withdraw consent ‚Äî Where processing is based on consent (e.g. notifications), you can withdraw at any time in app Settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@assettag.app. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK's data protection regulator, the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO), at ico.org.uk.

9 Children's Privacy

AssetTag is a business tool intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@assettag.app and we will delete it promptly.

10 Security

We take the security of your data seriously:

  • All communication between the app and cloud storage APIs (Microsoft Graph, Google Drive) is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • Authentication tokens are stored in Android's encrypted keystore ‚Äî they are not accessible to other apps
  • Your asset Excel file is protected by the access controls of your Microsoft or Google account ‚Äî only people you grant access to can read it
  • We do not store your data on our own servers, which eliminates the risk of a centralised data breach affecting your asset records

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. If you discover a security vulnerability in AssetTag, please report it responsibly to security@assettag.app.
